8.1. TECHNICAL ASPECTS
A Virtual AIS AtoN should be considered lost after 15 minutes, unless updated on the shipborne navigational displays, as specified in IMO MSC.1/Circ.
1473 [9].
A lost target symbol, as depicted in IEC 62288 [31], should be shown on the AIS AtoN symbol.
This consists of two crossed solid lines centred on the target symbol.
Once acknowledged, the lost target symbol and its target symbol shall be removed from the display.
