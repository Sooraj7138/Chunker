1. INTRODUCTION
A vessel traffic service (VTS) is recognized internationally as a navigational safety measure in the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea, 1974, as amended (SOLAS) [1].
The Convention also states that Contracting Governments planning and implementing VTS shall, wherever possible, follow the guidelines developed by the International Maritime Organization (IMO).
