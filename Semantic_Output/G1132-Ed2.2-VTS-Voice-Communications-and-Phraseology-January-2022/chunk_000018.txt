5.1.2.7. Request
This indicates that the following message is asking for action from others with respect to the ship and signals that something is to be arranged or provided.
Request should not be used involving navigation or to modify the Convention on the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (COLREGs) [8].
