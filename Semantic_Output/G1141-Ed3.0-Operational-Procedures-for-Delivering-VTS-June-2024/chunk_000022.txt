4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Processes and procedures should be implemented to demonstrate that the responsibilities, practices, policies and procedures exercised by an authority to provide strategic direction, ensure objectives, manage risk and use resources responsibly and with accountability are monitored and evaluated on a routine basis.
This should include documentation relating to:

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT

• VTS Objectives – Procedures to monitor and assess that the objectives set for the VTS are met and regularly reported to management.
Aspects and considerations include:

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT

• Setting performance measures, including both positive (leading) and negative (lag) performance measures;

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Establishing a framework to regularly assess the performance measures; and

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Reporting to management.
4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Evaluation – Procedures to regularly carry out an evaluation to ensure that the VTS operational objectives have been met, and the problems identified and defined for implementing the VTS have been either alleviated or at least reduced to an acceptable level.
Aspects and considerations in undertaking the evaluation include:

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
•

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT

• The need for the VTS, which may include monitoring and identifying changes that may have occurred since the VTS was implemented or when previous evaluation was made regarding the volume of traffic and degree of risk;

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
An operational evaluation of the VTS; and

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
An evaluation of the operational objectives and the list of problems requiring attention.
4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
•

4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Policies – Procedures for maintaining policies associated with VTS, such as qualifications and training, compliance and enforcement.
4.1.11. ADMINISTRATIVE/ MANAGEMENT
Please note Guideline G1131 Setting and Measuring VTS Objectives [10] provides further guidance for competent authorities and VTS Authorities for setting objectives for a VTS and achieving the obligations associated with SOLAS regulation V/12 (Vessel Traffic Services) [11] and IMO Resolution A.1158(32)[2].

The VTS provider should have plans to address events such as:
