No.
Issue/question 
1. Do you have any measures in place for identifying and managing opportunities for improvement?
For example, do you conduct customer satisfaction surveys with your stakeholders?

2. Do you have a process for taking corrective and/or preventative action as part of continually improving of the management system?

3. What performance measures are in place to assess and monitor that the objectives of the VTS are being met?
For example, do you collect any data on the number of accident/incidents such as groundings, collisions, allisions, near-misses, and pro-active interventions by VTS personnel?

4. Any other issues to discuss?
