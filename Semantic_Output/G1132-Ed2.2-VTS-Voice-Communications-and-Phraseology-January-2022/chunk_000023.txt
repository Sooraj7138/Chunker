5.1.7. COURSE
Course Made That course which a ship has made good over ground, as a result of the effect of currents, Good tidal streams and leeway.
Course to Make That course which a ship is to make good over ground, after allowing for the effect of Good currents, tidal streams, and leeway.
Track The path followed between one position and another.
Planned Track The path to be followed between one position and another.
Heading The horizontal direction of the vessel's bows at a given moment measured in degrees clockwise from True North.
