5.3.3. INFLUENCE OF INTERNAL AND EXTERNAL FACTORS
Some factors such as mental and emotional state, health, culture, working environment, distractions etc.
can influence the interpretation of communications.
Efforts should be made to minimize their negative effects on communications.
