5.1.8. DISTANCES
To be expressed in nautical miles or cables (tenths of a nautical mile), the unit is always to be stated.
5.1.9. SPEED
To be expressed in knots (nautical mile per hour).
5.1.9. SPEED
‘Speed’ refers to speed through the water.
If speed over the ground is intended, then this should be stated as ‘Speed over the Ground’ (SOG).
