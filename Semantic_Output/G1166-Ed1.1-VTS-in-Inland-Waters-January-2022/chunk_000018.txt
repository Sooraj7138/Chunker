4.13.1. IALA GUIDANCE
Where a formally appointed VTS is established, a quality management structure is considered necessary.
IALA provides guidance on quality management in the form of:

4.13.1. IALA GUIDANCE
Recommendation R0132 Quality Management for Aids to Navigation Authorities; and

4.13.1. IALA GUIDANCE
an associated Guideline G1052 Quality Management Systems for Aids to Navigation Service Delivery.
4.13.2. INLAND WATERS CONSIDERATIONS
An inland VTS should have a quality management system in place where auditing should form part of such a process.
The IALA guidance may be adapted as appropriate, taking into account relevant national, regional and local requirements.
