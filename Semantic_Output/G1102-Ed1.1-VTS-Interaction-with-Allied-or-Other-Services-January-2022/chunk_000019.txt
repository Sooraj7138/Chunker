C.9. NATURAL DISASTER
Natural disaster recovery procedures may be established to respond to situations such as earthquake, tsunami, tidal wave, fire, and exceptional weather conditions.
Actions may include:

C.9. NATURAL DISASTER
Promulgate information to vessels in the VTS area.
