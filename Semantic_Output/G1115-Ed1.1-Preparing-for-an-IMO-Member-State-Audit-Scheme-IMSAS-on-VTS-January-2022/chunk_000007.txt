RESOLUTION A.1067(28) ON THE FRAMEWORK AND PROCEDURES FOR THE IMO MEMBER STATE 4.1.1. AUDIT SCHEME

4.1. IMO
The purpose of this framework is to describe the objective, principles, scope, responsibilities and capacity-building aspect of the IMO Member State audit, which together constitute the strategy for the audit scheme.
4.1. IMO
This framework is supported by the procedures for the IMO Member State audit and the IMO .
