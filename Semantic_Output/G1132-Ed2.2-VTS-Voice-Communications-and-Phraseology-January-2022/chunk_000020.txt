5.1.3. PHONETIC ALPHABET
The phonetic alphabet is used to distinguish between letters, which sound similar when transmitted over the radio.
They are commonly used when transmitting call signs and in cases where a single letter is used to designate something.

5.1.4. PHONETIC NUMBERS [NUMERALS]
Numbers are to be spoken in separate digits.
For example:

5.1.4. PHONETIC NUMBERS [NUMERALS]
“ wun fife zeero” for 150
