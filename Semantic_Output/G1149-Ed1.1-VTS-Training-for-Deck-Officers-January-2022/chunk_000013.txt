6.6. FAMILIARIZATION ACTIVITIES
Familiarization activities may include visiting a VTS centre or arranging for a presentation from a representative of a VTS.
The objective of the familiarization is to explain the purpose of VTS to the deck officer, thereby enhancing VTS awareness.

FAMILIARIZATION BETWEEN ALLIED SERVICES AND VTS
6.7.

FAMILIARIZATION BETWEEN ALLIED SERVICES AND VTS
The involvement of allied services, including pilots, in the work of the VTS will promote the safety of navigation in the VTS area.
Therefore, the above familiarization activities may also be of benefit for the providers of such allied services.
