The following typographical conventions are used throughout this part to clearly emphasize the recommended phrases: ( ) brackets indicate that the part of the message enclosed within the brackets may be added where relevant; [ ] square brackets indicate optional content; oblique strokes indicate that the items on either side of the stroke are alternatives; / dots indicate that the relevant information is to be filled in where the dots occur; (italic letters) indicate the kind of information requested; and ~ tildes precede possible words or phrases which can be used after/in association with the given standard phrase.
