Operational considerations Compliance and Enforcement The competent authority for VTS should establish a compliance and enforcement framework with respect to violations of VTS regulatory requirements (Refer A.1158(32) 5.2.4) The VTS provider should “[Ensure] compliance with and enforcement of regulatory provisions for which they are empowered” (Refer G1089 4.2) IALA Standards Contracting Governments are encouraged to take into account IALA standards and associated recommendations, guidelines and model courses (Refer A.XXX(XX) Section 9) Ensuring that vessels which are not complying with VTS regulatory requirements are notified that they are breaking national law by means of formal process.
Demonstrate ongoing compliance with IALA Standards related to VTS, including: 
• 1040 ‐ Vessel Traffic Services; 
• 1010 ‐ AtoN Planning and Service Requirements; 
• 1050 ‐ Training and Certification; and 
• 1070 – Information Services.

• 100% violation actions taken and notifications issued to the contravening party.

• Regular internal and external audits confirm compliance.
