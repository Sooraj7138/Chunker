C.1. OPERATIONAL EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
The VTS functions according to IALA Guideline G1141 Operational Procedures for Delivering a VTS [2] are subdivided into internal and external.
External Procedures cover procedures that govern the interaction with participating vessels and allied services.
The VTS should be maintained during any emergency response.
C.1. OPERATIONAL EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
Incidental co-operation with emergency services, such as Search and Rescue and Pollution Control may be conducted in accordance with pre-established contingency plans in which the procedures for interaction with allied or other services are laid down and responsibilities established.
