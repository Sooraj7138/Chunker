4.5. ADMINISTRATION
Management activities that support the daily administration of a VTS may include:

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• Communication/public relations:

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• promoting, protecting and building the reputation of the VTS among its stakeholders, the media and the public; and

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• managing media and public relation issues.

Regulatory and legal guidance:

obtaining and providing advice on regulatory and legal aspects of VTS matters;

4.5. ADMINISTRATION
provide input to the regulatory and nautical matters concerning the VTS; and

4.5. ADMINISTRATION
compliance and enforcement of regulatory provisions.
4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• Quality/performance activities:

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• maintaining a quality management system including the preparation and facilitation of VTS audits;

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• assessing performance measures to ensure VTS objectives are being met and these are regularly reported to management; and

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• routinely evaluating that the VTS operational objectives are being met, and that the problems identified and defined for implementing the VTS have been mitigated to an acceptable level.
4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• Governance activities:

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• ensuring the VTS conforms with the regulatory framework set by the competent authority for VTS; and

4.5. ADMINISTRATION

• monitoring financial resources.
