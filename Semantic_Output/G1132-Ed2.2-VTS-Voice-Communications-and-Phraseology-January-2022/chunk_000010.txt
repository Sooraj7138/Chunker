4.5. CULTURAL DIFFERENCES
Differing cultural experiences and backgrounds may result in different responses to situations.
A lack of awareness of these differences could increase the possibility of errors and misunderstandings.
4.5. CULTURAL DIFFERENCES
When VTS personnel communicate cross-culturally special attention should be made to:

4.5. CULTURAL DIFFERENCES

• Share crucial information with ships to create a common perception of potential dangers, even if this information seems “obvious”.
4.5. CULTURAL DIFFERENCES

• Use closed loop (or Read-back) techniques when information may be misunderstood such as the number of persons on-board or information that would benefit others using the VTS area, instructions or advice.
