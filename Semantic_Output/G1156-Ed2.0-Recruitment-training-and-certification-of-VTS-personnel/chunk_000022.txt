VTS CAREER PROGRESSION
The formal recognition of VTS qualifications provides the foundation for a professional framework similar to that adopted by the shipping and pilotage industry.
The regular revalidation of these qualifications seeks to create quality standards comparable to other professions.
VTS CAREER PROGRESSION
VTS personnel have the scope for career progression by making use of the skills and experience gained, in either VTS, or other maritime environments (see example in Figure 2).
