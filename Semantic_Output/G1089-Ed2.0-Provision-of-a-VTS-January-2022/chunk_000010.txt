4. PROVISION OF VTS TO PARTICIPATING SHIPS
IMO Resolution A.1158(32) states that:


4. PROVISION OF VTS TO PARTICIPATING SHIPS
“The purpose of a VTS is to contribute to safety of life at sea, improve the safety and efficiency of navigation and support the protection of the environment within a VTS area by mitigating the development of unsafe situations through:


4. PROVISION OF VTS TO PARTICIPATING SHIPS
1. providing timely and relevant information on factors that may influence ship movements and assist on-board decision making.

4. PROVISION OF VTS TO PARTICIPATING SHIPS
2. monitoring and managing ship traffic to ensure the safety and efficiency of ship movements.

4. PROVISION OF VTS TO PARTICIPATING SHIPS
3. responding to developing unsafe situations.”
