PURPOSE OF THE DOCUMENT
The purpose of this Guideline is to provide a framework for authorities to implement processes and procedures associated with the provision of VTS.
RELATIONSHIP TO OTHER DOCUMENTS
IALA Guideline G1141 Operational procedures for Vessel Traffic Services [3] is associated with Recommendation R0127 (V-127) VTS Operations, a normative provision of IALA Standard S1040 Vessel Traffic Services (VTS)[4].
To demonstrate compliance with the Recommendation, the provisions of this Guideline need to be implemented.
