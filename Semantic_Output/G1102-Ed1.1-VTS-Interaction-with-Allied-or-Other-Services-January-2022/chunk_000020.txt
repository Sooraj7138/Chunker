C.10. COASTAL ACCIDENTS
Coastal accident procedures with allied and other services should be established to deal with accidents involving swimmers or divers reported directly to the VTS by witnesses.
Actions may include:

C.10. COASTAL ACCIDENTS

• Inform MRCC.

• Inform relevant competent services.
