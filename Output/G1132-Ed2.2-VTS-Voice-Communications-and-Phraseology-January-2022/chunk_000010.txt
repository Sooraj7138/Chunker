REPORTING OF SHIP IDENTIFICATION AND PARTICULARS When entering the VTS area a ship’s identity and particulars should have been passed in advance.
If not, these may need to be clarified through an instruction to report or a question asking for specific details.
The message marker QUESTION would normally be used prior to these message elements.
