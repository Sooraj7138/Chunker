EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
The overarching purpose of a good portrayal is to improve safety and efficiency.
To reach this goal, the following non-exhaustive list of items may be considered when determining user requirements:

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A defined area of interest

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Information that is provided when and where needed

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Standardization of portrayal configuration, which supports consistent application of operational procedures

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Role definition capabilities to support different portrayal profiles, such as Operators, Supervisors, and/or other users as required

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Ergonomic HMI considerations with the goal of reducing and mitigating operator fatigue, distraction, and other factors that may negatively impact overall safety and efficiency

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Minimizing non-essential administrative tasks in order to support the ability of the operator to focus upon critical tasks

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Where possible, automatic validation of data integrity should occur prior to presentation

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
Minimal steps needed to navigate the shore-based equipment in order to retrieve the necessary information to assist in real-time decision making

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
In a portrayal, a distinction should be made between operational alarms (related to navigation safety) and system alarms (related to technical deficiencies with the operating system).
These alarms should be configured and displayed in an appropriate manner to ensure that they are relevant to users

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A log of warnings and alarms should be maintained and readily accessible to the user

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A portrayal should not restrict the innovation of equipment manufacturers and unnecessarily interfere with the individual operational and risk-based requirements of individual shore-based Authorities

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A portrayal may also contain considerations regarding system failures and recovery, and if possible/appropriately describe business continuity measures

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A portrayal should consider local regulations considering storage and access of data

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A portrayal should consider measures to be taken against unauthorized access to the system

EXAMPLES OF ITEMS THAT MAY CONTRIBUTE TO A GOOD PORTRAYAL
A portrayal should have some flexibility to consider advanced technologies that may be adopted in the future for AtoN requirements and management (ARM), ships, VTS, etc..
